  221257N 1183154E
             
航路備註:
1. 本航路僅供臺北飛航情報區起飛及廣州飛航情報區交管之航機使用。
2. 航行在BEBEM與ANPOG間，駕駛員應全程開啟雷達迴波器(Transponder)；雷達迴波器及TCAS/ACAS應運作正常。
3. 使用高度：
    a. 自廣州飛航情報區飛往臺北飛航情報區之航機使用FL290。
    b. 自臺北飛航情報區飛往廣州飛航情報區之航機使用FL280。
　
Route Remarks:
1. This route is only available for aircraft departing from Taipei FIR and aircraft transferring via Guangzhou FIR.
2. All aircraft operated between BEBEM and ANPOG shall be equipped with Transponder and TCAS/ACAS and maintain in operation.
3. Flight Level available :
    a. From Guangzhou FIR to Taipei FIR: FL290.
    b. From Taipei FIR to Guangzhou FIR: FL280.
