MOLKA (FIR BDRY)
  263931N 1240000E
             
航路註解：
1. 本區尚未驗證DME/DME涵蓋情況。
2. 本航路僅供東北向航機使用。
3. 本航路僅在雷達正常情況下使用，航管單位利用雷達監視使用本航路之航空器，並提供雷達隔離。
4. 每日0000-1200 及2300-2359 航機經由M750/ENVAR進入臺北飛航情報區不得使用飛航空層290。
5. 每日2000UTC至1300UTC，於ENVAR與SANAS間之航段，使用飛航空層290以下應經航管同意。
6. 到場航機得依航管指示下降低於飛航空層270。
7. 臺北飛航情報區內可使用之RVSM空層:FL290, FL310, FL330, FL350, FL370, FL390, FL410。
　
Route Remarks:
1. Validation of DME/DME coverage has not been conducted.
2. This route is for north-east bound only.
3. This route is applicable only under radar environment. ATC will monitor navigation performance, and radar separation will be provided.
4. FL290 not available for flights inbound Taipei FIR via M750/ENVAR between 0000-1200 & 2300-2359.
5. 2000-1300UTC daily, between ENVAR and SANAS, below FL290 shall be approved by ATC.
6. Arrival aircraft may descend below FL270 in accordance with ATC clearance.
7. Flight levels available in Taipei FIR RVSM airspace: FL290, FL310, FL330, FL350, FL370, FL390, FL410.
